Before getting started with AdRoll billing, we need to understand the following:
We charge for advertisers upfront, meaning that if the advertiser is launching two campaigns with $100 per week, we will hold that amount into AdRoll account and use that balance for that particular week. In case if the campaign has served $40 for that particular week, We forward that amount to the next billing cycle and we will make sure that advertiser is holding the required balance, before every starting of the billing period.

Lets understand first simple billing: If your account has two campaigns with budget $50 and $150 for a week, the amount we need in your account balance is $200.

Now, if your campaigns have served $5 and $30 in total $35, in that case, your account balance is holding an amount of ($200-$35)=$165. Which means your account is short $35 for the required balance. Hence, to replenish your account we charge $35

The total of your campaign budgets for the week is the amount needed in your balance, is your required balance.

Invoice: is the receipt generated by the AdRoll. You can find this under settings of billing information in AdRoll dashboard
Required Balance: is the sum of all weekly budget of the campaigns plus due amount.
Payment Made: Successful transaction of the amount paid
